# RevealUI Pricing Recommendations (2025 Market Analysis)

Based on current market data for comparable products, here are pricing strategies for licensing access to the RevealUI codebase.

## Market Context (2025)

### Comparable Products Pricing:
- **PayloadCMS**: Free (MIT) self-hosted, $35-199/month managed
- **Strapi**: Free self-hosted, $15-375/month cloud
- **Sanity**: Free tier, $15/seat/month Growth plan
- **Contentful**: Free tier, $300/month Lite, custom enterprise ($40-100k+/year)
- **Premium Boilerplates**: $80-$600 one-time, or $150-$500/month subscription
- **White-label Multi-tenant CMS**: $3k-$15k/year (small), $20k-$60k/year (mid), $100k+/year (enterprise)
- **Enterprise SaaS Licenses**: $14,700 one-time (Monocube example)

## RevealUI Unique Value Proposition

What makes RevealUI worth premium pricing:
1. ✅ **AI System Built-In**: Memory management, LLM providers, agent orchestration (not available in Payload/Strapi)
2. ✅ **E-Commerce Ready**: Stripe integration, Products/Orders/Prices collections
3. ✅ **Multi-Tenant by Default**: Built-in tenant isolation and management
4. ✅ **Real-time Sync**: Electric SQL for collaborative editing
5. ✅ **Full Source Code Access**: Complete customization vs. hosted-only solutions
6. ✅ **Production-Ready**: Auth, rate limiting, security, testing framework included

## Recommended Pricing Strategies

**Target**: Individual developers, small agencies, solopreneurs

| Tier | Price | What's Included | Use Case |
|------|-------|----------------|----------|
| **Starter** | **$497** (one-time) | Single developer license, 1 project, 1 year of updates, community support | Small projects, learning, MVPs |
| **Professional** | **$997** (one-time) | Single developer, unlimited projects, lifetime updates, email support | Freelancers, agencies with multiple clients |
| **Agency** | **$2,497** (one-time) | 5 developer seats, unlimited projects, lifetime updates, priority support, white-label rights | Small agencies reselling to clients |

**Why this works**: Lower barrier to entry, one-time payment appeals to indie hackers, competitive with premium boilerplates ($299-$600 range).

## Pricing Psychology Recommendations

### Use "Anchoring":
- **Show Enterprise tier first**: $9,997 makes $1,997 seem reasonable
- **Highlight value**: "Save $18,000 vs. 3 years of Contentful Lite"
- **Compare to development cost**: "Build this in-house: $50k-$200k+ and 6-12 months"

### Transparency Builds Trust:
- **Clear feature comparison table**: What you get vs. competitors
- **Case studies**: Show real ROI from customers
- **Trial/demo**: Let customers test before buying (14-day trial, limited features)

### Create Urgency:
- **Launch pricing**: "First 100 customers: 50% off"
- **Limited-time bonuses**: Free onboarding, custom development hours
- **Annual pricing discount**: "Pay annually, save 20%"
